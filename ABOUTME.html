About Us

icmForUs is a dynamic creative company founded on June 28, 2024, by a group of passionate students. Led by founder Gabriel Menghisteab and co-founders Awet Yacob, Daniel Menghisteab, and Nahom Kebede, the team brings together diverse talents in technology, media, and creativity. The company’s name reflects its core values, with “I” standing for innovation and “C” for creativity, embodying a mission to highlight and nurture groundbreaking achievements through multidisciplinary media.

icmForUs was conceptualized in June 2024, with a vision to create an organization where creativity and technology meet to foster innovation. Gabriel Menghisteab, a computer science major and a dedicated photographer, sought to use his skills to positively impact the world. Together with his partners, icmForUs became a platform to explore and develop projects that have the potential to influence the world, one step at a time.

The company’s mission is to showcase innovative and creative achievements on a global scale, aiming to leave a lasting mark through media. Each team member brings their unique expertise and vision to the table, driving forward the collective goal of producing work that matters. Whether through photography, technology, or creative media, icmForUs is dedicated to pushing boundaries and turning ideas into reality.
