ABOUT gliimpsse

gliimpsse is a personal portfolio website showcasing my journey as a second-year Computer Science major. It highlights my skills, projects, and interests, combining my passion for media design, software development, and photography. The site serves as a hub for my CS projects, reflecting my technical growth and creativity, while also featuring my photography work, where I offer bookings for photoshoots. Through this platform, I aim to elevate my presence in both fields, connecting with opportunities for internships, jobs, and creative collaborations. Whether you're looking to explore my coding projects or book a photoshoot, gliimpsse offers a glimpse into my evolving work.
