About Us

We are an organization for Eritrean and Ethiopian students at Georgia Tech. Our mission is to provide a safe and inclusive space for students to connect with their culture and develop a community. EESA also aims to share Eritrean and Ethiopian cultures and traditions within the larger Georgia Tech community.
