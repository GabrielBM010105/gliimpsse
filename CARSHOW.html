Rockdale Career Academy Car Show

May 12, 2024

A gallery capturing the 2024 annual car show at Rockdale Career Academy in Conyers, Rockdale County, GA. The event featured a diverse array of vehicles, from classic cars to modern exotics, drawing enthusiasts and community members alike. The show included live demonstrations, awards for best in class, and opportunities for attendees to interact with car owners and learn more about the automotive industry. The gallery highlights the vibrant atmosphere, showcasing the detailed craftsmanship of the cars and the excitement of the crowd. All pictures taken by Daniel Menghisteab.
