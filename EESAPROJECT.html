Georgia Tech Eritrean-Ethiopian Website

May 27, 2024

The first project for ICM involved developing a website for Georgia Tech's Eritrean-Ethiopian Student Association (EESA). Completed by Gabriel Menghisteab in late May, the site features a Home page, an eBoard members page, a photo gallery showcasing pictures from each event and meeting, an upcoming events page, and an information page. The home page features a slideshow at the top, highlighting several meetings from the 2023-2024 year, with the organization's name and logo displayed above it. Below, there is a section for announcements and upcoming meetings, keeping users informed about future events. The footer, present on every page, provides a brief overview of the organization along with contact information. The eBoard members page lists all positions, includes photos of each member, and highlights their roles. The photo gallery is organized by year and event, allowing users to click on images for a closer view. The upcoming events page details future events and meetings, complete with a calendar. Lastly, the info page reiterates the content found in the footer. The website will be published once permission is granted by Georgia Tech EESA.
