How To Book

On-campus students interested in booking a session should reach out to us via email with their preferred dates and times. We will check availability and confirm your appointment via email.

Payment is required in person on the day of your session, and both cash and card options are accepted. If you have any specific requests or need additional information, please include these details in your email.
