Personal Portfolios

August 16, 2024

Each ICM member (with the excepttion of Daniel Menghisteab) will create a personal page on the company's website that showcases their unique ambitions, talents, and personalities. These pages will serve as a platform for each individual to highlight their personal goals, career aspirations, and contributions to the team, while also offering insight into their creative styles and professional growth. By doing so, the pages will not only emphasize the diverse skill sets within the group but also allow potential collaborators, clients, and followers to better understand and connect with each member on a deeper level. All three sites are expected to be completed and published by October 4.
