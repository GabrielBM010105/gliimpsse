Parties

Our party photoshoots are designed to capture the joy and excitement of your special celebrations. Whether you're hosting a birthday bash, anniversary party, or any other festive event, we are skilled at documenting every memorable moment. From candid shots of guests enjoying themselves to carefully staged group photos, we ensure that your event is beautifully preserved. Contact us to discuss your party details, and we’ll work with you to create a tailored photography experience that fits your vision.

How To Book

To book a photoshoot for an event, first contact the photographer via email or phone with details about the event, including the date, time, and location. Share any specific requirements or themes you have in mind. After discussing the specifics, you’ll receive a quote and finalize the arrangements. On the day of the event, the photographer will arrive early to set up and ensure they capture all important moments. Payment is generally made in person, with both cash and online card options are accepted, including Zelle, CashApp, and Apple Pay.

Prices

When booking a party photoshoot, the price for events within a 15-mile radius is $100, which includes up to two hours of coverage and digital copies of all edited photos. If your event requires coverage beyond the initial two hours, an hourly rate of $50 will apply for any additional time. This ensures you get the full coverage you need without any rush. For events located beyond this radius, the price sets at $150.

The package includes professional photos of your event, digital copies of all edited images, and an online gallery posted on our site (optional). For more information or to book your photoshoot, feel free to reach out via email or phone. We’re here to help make your event memorable with high-quality photos tailored to your needs.
